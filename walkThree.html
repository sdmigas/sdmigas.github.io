Pakahakaha Bay Walk

Whangamata

Trail Information

Try this 2.1-km out-and-back trail near Whangamata, Waikato. Generally considered a moderately challenging route, it takes an average of 55 min to complete. This trail is great for hiking and walking, and it's unlikely you'll encounter many other people while exploring. The best times to visit this trail are November through April.
